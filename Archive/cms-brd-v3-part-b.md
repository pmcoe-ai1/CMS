### 5.9 Multi-Tenancy & Tenant Management

The system must support multi-tenancy, where multiple independent organisations (tenants) share a single CMS installation while maintaining complete isolation of their users, content, websites, configurations, and data. Each tenant operates as if it has its own dedicated CMS instance. The Platform Administrator role operates above the tenant level and is the only role with cross-tenant visibility.
